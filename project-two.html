Project #2:Teametrix

Product: Teametrix

Role: Software Developer

Duration: Sept 2019 to Aug 2020

Description: This project aim is to provide a platform for the company to recruit fresh candidates. Here the recruiter would be able to schedule an interview for a candidate (either through an online interview - face to face or through offline with defined set of questions). And at the scheduled time, the candidate can attend the online interview from his location or else he can record the answers to the corresponding questions displayed if it is offline. The panel member will give his feedback to admin and the final decision is made by admin. There is an additional option provided for the candidate to upload his/her resume and maintain his profile so that the recruiter can invite him/her for an interview.
